A Family Campground Worth Coming Back To

Bear Lake North has been owned and operated by the Wilkes family since 2003. Located in St. Charles on the east side of Highway 89, we're the closest campground to Bear Lake's famous turquoise waters.

Whether you're pulling in a rig, pitching a tent, or settling into one of our cabins, you'll find clean facilities, friendly people, and everything you need to make the most of Bear Lake country.

  • ✓ Under 10 minutes to Bear Lake's North Beach
  • ✓ 25 minutes to Minnetonka Cave
  • ✓ ATV trail access into Caribou-Targhee National Forest
  • ✓ Laundry, coin-op showers, and on-site amenities
  • ✓ Leashed pets welcome

Things to Do Near Bear Lake

You won't run out of ways to fill the days.

🏖️

Bear Lake North Beach

Famous turquoise water that rivals the Caribbean — just 10 minutes from camp. Sandy shore, swimming, and summer vibes.

Day-use reservations → Visitor info →
⛵

Watercraft Rentals

Kayaks, paddleboards, and boat rentals are available right on the lake — a perfect way to spend an afternoon.

Bear Lake Fun → Epic Recreation →
🦇

Minnetonka Cave

A stunning limestone cavern with guided tours through summer — only 25 minutes away. A must for families.

🏍️

ATV & OHV Trails

Access the trail network in Caribou-Targhee National Forest right from camp. Miles of riding through open country.

🎣

Fishing

Bear Lake is home to the Bonneville cisco — a fish found nowhere else on earth — plus cutthroat trout and whitefish.

🏛️

Oregon Trail History

The National Oregon/California Trail Center in nearby Montpelier brings frontier history to life for all ages.
