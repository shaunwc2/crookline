Rules & Policies

"Be considerate of your fellow campers, don't walk through their site."

Check-In & Check-Out

  • Check-in: 2:00 p.m.
  • Check-out: 11:00 a.m.
  • Quiet hours: 10:00 p.m. – 8:00 a.m. All children must be at their sites; day guests must leave.

Site Occupancy

  • Standard RV sites: maximum 6 people per site
  • Tent sites: maximum 5 people per site
  • Children under 10 do not count toward occupancy limits
  • Tents are not permitted on standard RV sites
  • One tent is included with each tent site; additional tents are $15/night each
  • Most tent sites are limited to 2 tents maximum

Parking

  • All overnight vehicles must display a parking pass
  • Tent sites: one car allowed per tent (max 2 tents = max 2 cars)
  • RV sites: one extra vehicle allowed beyond the tow vehicle; extra vehicle fee is $5/night
  • Visitors must park in the overflow lot and must leave by 10:00 p.m.

Fires

  • Wood fires are permitted only on tent sites and premium RV sites, in designated fire pits only
  • All fires must be fully extinguished by midnight
  • Propane fire pits are available to rent from the office

Generators

  • Generators are not permitted except during power outages

Sewer & Utilities

  • Sewer hoses must use a collar at the connection point
  • Do not put paper towels, feminine products, or rubber gloves down sewer pipes
  • Guests with medical electricity needs ($3/night) must notify the office in advance

Pets

  • Pets must remain leashed and quiet at all times
  • Use the designated pet walking area only
  • All waste must be picked up immediately

Speed, Noise & Safety

  • 5 mph speed limit enforced throughout the park
  • No ATVs or off-road vehicles on park roads (local trails permitted)
  • No fireworks of any kind
  • No smoking in buildings or on the playground
  • Alcohol must remain confined to individual campsites
  • Garbage goes in the dumpster only — do not leave bags outside

Enforcement

  • Management reserves the right to ask guests violating park rules to leave without refund

Reservation Policies

Making a Reservation

  • Reservations can be made online 24/7 or by phone at (208) 945-2941 from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m.
  • First night's deposit per site is required — reservation is not confirmed until payment is received
  • A confirmation number will be issued once payment is processed
  • Online system limits: 15 minutes to complete, max 4 bookings/hour, max 6 sites/booking, max 14 nights/booking

Site Assignment

  • Specific site numbers are not guaranteed — site type only
  • We will do our best to honor requests, but cannot guarantee site numbers during peak season

No-Show Policy

  • If we have not heard from you by 9:00 p.m. on your arrival day, your reservation will be cancelled
  • For multi-night stays, the second night's rate will be charged as a cancellation fee
  • Refunds are processed within 10 days

Cancellation Fees

Cancellation requests must be emailed to cancel@bearlakenorth.com. Check-in time (2:00 p.m.) on your arrival date is the reference point for all calculations.

Notice Given Before Arrival Fee Per Site
30 or more days $5
10 or more days $10
2 or more days Half of deposit
Less than 48 hours Full deposit
