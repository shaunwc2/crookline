💳 Deposit

First night's rate per site is required to confirm your reservation. You'll receive a confirmation number once payment is processed.

We guarantee your site type but not a specific site number. We'll do our best to honor requests, especially during peak season.

❌ Cancellations

Email cancel@bearlakenorth.com. Fees range from $5 (30+ days out) to full deposit (under 48 hours). See full policy →

⏰ No-Shows

If we haven't heard from you by 9:00 p.m. on your arrival day, your reservation will be cancelled and the second night charged as a fee.
