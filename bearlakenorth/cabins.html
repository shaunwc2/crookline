Furnished Cabins

Everything you need — no tent, no gear, just show up and relax.

About the Cabins

We have three small one-room cabins available for rent. Each cabin is fully furnished — just bring yourself and your food. Linens, dishes, and a small TV are all provided.

The cabins sleep comfortably for a couple or small family, with a full bed and single bunk, plus a carpeted sleeping loft above the bathroom that has room for several sleeping bags.

🛏 Sleeping

Full bed with single bunk above; carpeted loft for sleeping bags

🍳 Kitchen

2-burner stovetop, microwave, mini fridge, sink, dishes included

🚿 Bathroom

Private shower and toilet in each cabin

📺 Extras

Small TV, linens provided, ceiling fan

$145 / night + tax
